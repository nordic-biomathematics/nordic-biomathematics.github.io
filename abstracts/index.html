Call for Abstracts

Abstract submission is not yet open. More information will be added closer to the meeting.

Contributed Talks

We invite researchers at all career stages to submit abstracts for contributed talks. Contributed talks will be selected by the Scientific Committee based on scientific quality and overall programme balance.

  • Selection is made by the Scientific Committee.
  • Criteria include scientific quality and balance across topics and career stages.
  • Notifications will be sent after the submission deadline.

Poster Session

The meeting will include a poster session. Posters are welcome from all participants. Each poster presenter will have the opportunity to give a brief 1-minute lightning talk to introduce their poster to the full audience.

Further Information

Details on abstract length, format, submission form, and notification dates will be published on this page in due course. If you have questions in the meantime, please contact the organisers.
