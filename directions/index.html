Venue

The 3rd Nordic Biomathematics Meeting will be held at Ångströmlaboratoriet, Uppsala University, Uppsala, Sweden.

Ångströmlaboratoriet is one of Uppsala University’s main research buildings for natural sciences and technology, located on the Polacksbacken campus south of the city centre.

Getting to Uppsala

From Stockholm Arlanda Airport

Uppsala is well connected to Stockholm Arlanda Airport (ARN), which is the closest international airport. The journey takes approximately 20–30 minutes by train.

  • By train: Direct trains run frequently between Arlanda Airport and Uppsala Central Station. Trains are operated by SJ and Flygbussarna. The journey takes around 18–25 minutes. Tickets can be purchased online at sj.se or at the station.
  • By bus: Flygbussarna operates direct buses from Arlanda to Uppsala city centre. The journey takes around 40–50 minutes depending on traffic. See flygbussarna.se for schedules.

From Stockholm City

Uppsala is approximately 70 km north of Stockholm. Frequent trains run between Stockholm Central Station and Uppsala Central Station (journey time approximately 40 minutes).

From Uppsala Central Station to the Venue

Ångströmlaboratoriet is located about 2 km from Uppsala Central Station. It can be reached by local bus (several routes, see ul.se) or by a 25-minute walk or short taxi ride.

Accommodation

Suggestions for nearby hotels in Uppsala will be added to this page closer to the meeting. Uppsala city centre has a range of hotels at various price points, most within easy reach of the venue.
