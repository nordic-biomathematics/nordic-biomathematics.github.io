The 3rd Nordic Biomathematics Meeting

3–4 December 2026  ·  Ångströmlaboratoriet, Uppsala University  ·  Uppsala, Sweden

The meeting brings together researchers in mathematical biology and biomathematics across the Nordic countries. It aims to support scientific exchange, strengthen existing collaborations, and catalyse new ones across institutions, research areas, and career stages.

Key Information

Dates 3–4 December 2026
Location Ångströmlaboratoriet, Uppsala University, Uppsala, Sweden
Organisers Sara Hamis (Uppsala University) and Linnéa Gyllingberg (KTH Royal Institute of Technology)
Registration Registration will open in August 2026 — participation is free of charge
