Organisers

The 3rd Nordic Biomathematics Meeting is organised by:

  • Sara Hamis — Uppsala University, Sweden
  • Linnéa Gyllingberg — KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden

For enquiries, please contact the organisers at sara.hamis@math.uu.se or linneag@kth.se.

Scientific Committee

The scientific programme is overseen by an international Scientific Committee representing the Nordic countries:

  • Morten Andersson — Roskilde University, Denmark
  • Moriah Echlin — Tampere University, Finland
  • Einar Bjarki Gunnarsson — University of Iceland, Iceland
  • Alvaro Köhn-Luque — University of Oslo and Oslo University Hospital, Norway
  • Philip Gerlee — Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden

Acknowledgements

The 3rd Nordic Biomathematics Meeting is supported by the Wenner-Gren Foundations. The meeting is hosted at Ångströmlaboratoriet, Uppsala University.
