Programme

Preliminary programme. The programme is subject to change. Contributed speaker details will be added following abstract selection.

The meeting features 5 plenary talks, 14 contributed talks, a poster session with 1-minute lightning talks, coffee breaks, lunches, and a symposium dinner on the evening of 3 December.

Thursday 3 December 2026
09:00 Registration and Welcome Coffee
Arrival and registration at Ångströmlaboratoriet.
09:30 Opening Remarks
Welcome by the organisers.
09:45 Plenary Talk 1
Susanne Ditlevsen — University of Copenhagen
Statistical inference for stochastic processes in biology
10:45 Coffee Break
11:15 Contributed Talks — Session 1 (3 talks)
Speakers to be announced following abstract selection.
12:30 Lunch
14:00 Plenary Talk 2
Stefano Sarao Mannelli — Chalmers University of Technology and University of Gothenburg
Theoretical machine learning for understanding neural systems
15:00 Contributed Talks — Session 2 (3 talks)
Speakers to be announced following abstract selection.
16:15 Coffee Break
16:30 Poster Session with Lightning Talks
Each poster presenter gives a 1-minute lightning talk to introduce their work, followed by the full poster session.
19:00 Symposium Dinner
Venue to be confirmed.
Friday 4 December 2026
09:00 Plenary Talk 3
Susanna Röblitz — University of Bergen
Computational modelling of high-dimensional systems biology
10:00 Coffee Break
10:30 Contributed Talks — Session 3 (4 talks)
Speakers to be announced following abstract selection.
12:10 Lunch
13:30 Plenary Talk 4
Eric Libby — Umeå University
Mathematical models of major evolutionary transitions
14:30 Contributed Talks — Session 4 (4 talks)
Speakers to be announced following abstract selection.
16:10 Coffee Break
16:30 Plenary Talk 5  Nordic-language talk
Mats Gyllenberg — Finnish Society of Sciences and Letters and University of Helsinki
Population structure and adaptive dynamics in ecology
17:30 Closing Remarks
Closing of the meeting by the organisers.
