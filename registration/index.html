Registration

Registration will open in August 2026.

Registration is free of charge.

Participation

Participation in the 3rd Nordic Biomathematics Meeting is free of charge. This includes attendance at all sessions, coffee breaks, and lunches. The symposium dinner on the evening of 3 December is also included.

The meeting is open to all researchers with an interest in mathematical biology and biomathematics, at any career stage.

How to Register

A registration form will be made available on this page in August 2026. Registered participants will be asked to confirm their attendance closer to the meeting to assist with catering planning.

Places may be limited. Early registration is encouraged.

If you have questions about registration, please contact the organisers.
