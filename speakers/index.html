Plenary Speakers

The meeting features five invited plenary talks from leading researchers across the Nordic region, covering a broad range of topics in mathematical biology and biomathematics. One plenary talk will be delivered in a Nordic language as a special feature of the meeting.

Susanne Ditlevsen

University of Copenhagen, Denmark

Topic: Statistical inference for stochastic processes in biology

Stefano Sarao Mannelli

Chalmers University of Technology and University of Gothenburg, Sweden

Topic: Theoretical machine learning for understanding neural systems

Susanna Röblitz

University of Bergen, Norway

Topic: Computational modelling of high-dimensional systems biology

Eric Libby

Umeå University, Sweden

Topic: Mathematical models of major evolutionary transitions

Mats Gyllenberg Nordic-language talk

Finnish Society of Sciences and Letters and University of Helsinki, Finland

Topic: Population structure and adaptive dynamics in ecology
